## Long Description
*(Plain text, no markdown — Chrome Web Store renders this as-is)*

JobLink is a Chrome Side Panel extension for serious job seekers. Capture job postings from any website with one click, review and edit them without leaving the page, and save everything to your own Google Drive — organised, searchable, and always yours.

──────────────────────────────
FREE FEATURES
──────────────────────────────

ONE-CLICK JOB CAPTURE
Open a job posting on LinkedIn, Indeed, or any company careers page. JobLink automatically extracts the job title, company, location, and full description into the Side Panel. Edit any field before saving.

SAVE TO GOOGLE DRIVE
Every saved job creates a folder in your Drive containing four files: a structured JSON record, a formatted HTML summary, a PDF, and a Google Doc — all named and organised automatically.

STATUS TRACKING
Jobs move through three stages: Preparation (interested), Submitted (applied), and Rejected. JobLink creates these folders in your Drive automatically and keeps everything sorted.

IN-EXTENSION DASHBOARD
A full job management dashboard lets you sort and filter all your saved jobs by status, company, location, date, salary, or job type. Click any job to open a detail panel with notes, all Drive document links, and quick status actions. Select multiple jobs for bulk moves or bulk reject.

DUPLICATE DETECTION
JobLink checks your Drive whenever you open a job page and warns you if you've already saved that role — so you never apply twice by accident.

WORKS EVERYWHERE
Dedicated scrapers for LinkedIn and Indeed, plus a smart generic scraper for any company careers page, Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, and more.

──────────────────────────────
PRO FEATURES ($4.99/month)
──────────────────────────────

Pro requires at least one AI provider API key (Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google Gemini) or a JobLink Pro licence key.

EVALUATE FIT
Get an AI-powered match score (0–100) for any job against your candidate profile. See a breakdown of what matches, what's missing, and a plain-English recommendation on whether to apply.

PREPARE PACKAGE
Generate a tailored CV and cover letter in your own Google Doc templates — preserving all your formatting, fonts, and layout — with AI-written content specific to the role. Documents save directly to your Drive's Submitted folder.

ACADEMIC PACKAGE
For academic job applications: generates CV, Cover Letter, Research Statement, Diversity Statement, and Teaching Statement in one click.

MULTI-MODEL SUPPORT
Choose the AI model that fits your budget and quality needs: Claude Sonnet or Haiku (Anthropic), Gemini 2.5 Pro or Flash (Google), GPT-4o, GPT-4o Mini, GPT-4 Turbo, o1, or o3-mini (OpenAI).

──────────────────────────────
YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
──────────────────────────────

JobLink has no backend server. All job data is saved to your own Google Drive account — the developer cannot see it. AI API keys are stored locally in Chrome and sent only to the AI provider you choose. No analytics or usage data is ever collected.

──────────────────────────────
REQUIREMENTS
──────────────────────────────

• Chrome 114 or later
• A Google account (for Drive storage)
• For Pro features: an API key from Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google, or a JobLink Pro licence key
