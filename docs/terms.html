2. Description of Service

JobLink is a Chrome browser extension that helps you manage your job search. It provides the following functionality:

  • Scraping and capturing job postings from LinkedIn, Indeed, and other careers pages you visit
  • Saving structured job data (JSON, HTML, PDF) to your personal Google Drive account
  • A kanban-style dashboard for tracking application status (Preparation, Submitted, Rejected)
  • AI-assisted tools for evaluating job fit, tailoring CVs, and drafting cover letters (Pro tier)

JobLink is an AI-powered job application assistant. It does not submit applications on your behalf and does not interact with job boards beyond reading the page you are currently viewing.
