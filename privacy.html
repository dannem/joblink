JobLink — Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — the short answer is: we don't. Your data stays yours.

Summary JobLink has no backend server and collects no data. Everything you save goes directly into your own Google Drive. API keys you enter are stored only in your browser and sent directly to the respective AI provider. We never see your data.

1. What Data JobLink Accesses

JobLink accesses the following data locally on your device or on services you control:

Data Where it comes from Where it goes
Job posting content (title, company, location, description, URL) Scraped from the page you are viewing (LinkedIn, Indeed, or any careers page) Your Google Drive only
Google OAuth token Chrome Identity API (you sign in via Google's own consent screen) Used locally to call the Google Drive API on your behalf — never stored on our servers
AI provider API keys (Anthropic, Google Gemini, OpenAI) Entered by you in the extension settings Stored in chrome.storage.sync in your browser; sent directly to the respective AI provider when you use AI features — never transmitted to JobLink
Profile documents (CV, cover letter templates) Files you select from your Google Drive Read locally and sent to the AI provider you have configured — never to JobLink

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Job data is written to a folder in your own Google Drive that you configure during setup. JobLink does not read, copy, or transmit this data anywhere else.
  • AI features (CV tailoring, cover letter generation, job evaluation) send the job description and your profile documents directly from your browser to the AI provider you have chosen. This is a direct browser-to-provider connection — JobLink is not in the middle.
  • API keys are used only to authenticate those direct requests to AI providers. They are never logged, transmitted to JobLink, or shared with any third party other than the provider you configured.
  • No analytics, no tracking, no advertising. We do not use cookies, pixel trackers, session recording, or any form of analytics.

3. Third-Party Services

JobLink integrates with the following third-party services. Your use of these services is subject to their own privacy policies.

Service Purpose Data sent
Google Drive API Save and retrieve job files Job data you save; files you read for AI generation. All data goes into your own Drive account.
Anthropic API CV tailoring, cover letter generation, job evaluation (if Anthropic model selected) Job description, your profile documents. Only when you explicitly trigger an AI action.
Google Gemini API Same as above (if Gemini model selected) Same as above.
OpenAI API Same as above (if OpenAI model selected) Same as above.

JobLink does not share your data with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Google Drive — all job files (JSON, HTML, PDF documents) live in your Drive. You can delete them at any time from within Drive.
  • Browser storage (chrome.storage.sync) — extension settings (folder IDs, API keys, preferences) are stored in your browser and synced via your Google account if Chrome sync is enabled. This data never passes through JobLink servers.
  • Uninstalling JobLink removes all locally stored settings. Files already saved to your Google Drive are not affected and remain in your Drive until you delete them.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

JobLink has no backend server. We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • The content of job postings you save
  • Your CV, cover letter, or any profile documents
  • Your API keys or OAuth tokens
  • Usage statistics, feature telemetry, or crash reports
  • IP addresses or device identifiers

6. Children's Privacy

JobLink is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date below and, where appropriate, note the change in the extension's update release notes.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@joblink.app.
